POLICY

We built YSKAIPE so homeowners could stop getting ripped off. That mission only works if you trust us with your information. Here's exactly how we handle it.

Effective date: March 26, 2026 · yskaipe.com
The short version: We collect only what we need to give you accurate quotes. We don't sell your data. We don't run ads. We don't share your information with contractors without your consent. YSKAIPE exists to give you power — not to monetize your vulnerability.

Who We Are

YSKAIPE ("we," "us," "our") is a home services cost intelligence platform based in North Carolina. Our mission is to give every homeowner the knowledge they need to make fair, informed decisions about home repair and improvement — without being taken advantage of.

We can be reached at gr8@yskaipe.com with any privacy-related questions.

What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to deliver your quote and improve our service:

  • Job information — trade type, ZIP code, home size, and your description of the job. This is used to generate your cost estimate.
  • Optional contact details — your name and email, only if you choose to provide them for quote delivery. Never required.
  • Usage data — anonymized analytics about how the tool is used (page views, quote types, general usage patterns). No personal identifiers attached.

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, contractor account credentials, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your instant standard cost estimate
  • To deliver your quote via email if you request it
  • To improve the accuracy of our AI-powered pricing models
  • To understand how people use YSKAIPE so we can make it better

We will never use your information to target you with advertising, sell you additional services without your consent, or share identifiable data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Claude AI & Anthropic

Powered by Claude · Anthropic

YSKAIPE uses Claude, Anthropic's AI, as our core intelligence engine. When you submit a quote request, your job description is sent to Anthropic's API to generate pricing and DIY guidance. This data is processed under Anthropic's privacy policy and is not used to train their models without consent. We chose Claude because Anthropic's commitment to safe, honest, and beneficial AI aligns directly with YSKAIPE's mission of transparency and consumer protection. You can review Anthropic's privacy practices at anthropic.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Period.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., "most common job types in NC") for industry reporting or product improvement. This data cannot be traced back to any individual.

The only circumstances under which we would share identifiable information:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., connecting you to a vetted pro in our network)
  • If required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect the safety of our users or the public

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal, functional cookies only — no advertising trackers, no third-party behavioral tracking pixels. We use anonymized analytics to understand general usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time without affecting the core functionality of YSKAIPE.

Data Retention

Quote data submitted without an email address is not stored after your session ends. If you provide an email for quote delivery, we retain your quote data for 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted. You can request deletion at any time by emailing gr8@yskaipe.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at gr8@yskaipe.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

Children's Privacy

YSKAIPE is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our platform, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users by email. Continued use of YSKAIPE after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
