The Worker Network

Three Tiers.
One Platform.

YSKAIPE is the first home services marketplace to recognize that not every job needs a licensed contractor — and not every crew member has to wait years to earn. We match task complexity to worker tier, automatically.

Tier 01
🔧
Primary Workers
The Core Workforce

Skilled tradespeople and handymen with verified experience across our 8 Pro Core trades. The backbone of YSKAIPE — vetted, rated, and dispatched daily.

  • Verified trade experience across all 8 core categories
  • ZIP-code exclusive territory protection
  • AutoQuote pricing — no more haggling
  • Direct lead delivery, no shared bids
  • Fair Rate Index protects your earning floor
Tier 02
⚡
Licensed Pros
Certified. Insured. Premium.

State-licensed contractors for complex, code-required work. Electricians, master plumbers, HVAC engineers, structural roofers. The jobs that demand credentials.

  • License + insurance verification on file
  • Premium pricing tier — command what you're worth
  • Priority dispatch on high-value permits
  • Contractor dashboard with full lead lifecycle
  • Founding member program — lock your territory
Tier 03
🌱
Youth Workers
First Paycheck. Real Work.

Teens and young adults earning through lawn care, moving help, light landscaping, and home prep. YSKAIPE gives first-time workers a legitimate, safe entry into the trades economy.

  • Supervised job categories sized for entry-level
  • Transparent, fair wages — no exploitation
  • Skill-building ratings that follow them up tiers
  • Parent-approved job flow for minors
  • The on-ramp to the primary worker network
