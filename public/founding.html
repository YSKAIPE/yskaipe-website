Workflow

From homeowner request
to your phone, in seconds.

01

Homeowner submits a request

They describe the job on yskaipe.com. The platform instantly generates a Premium Auto-Quote — estimated cost breakdown, difficulty score, DIY shopping list, and pro options.

02

AI trade agent takes over

A dedicated agent for that trade (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, etc.) walks the homeowner through their options — while simultaneously running the 4-factor scoring algorithm against the contractor queue.

03

Best-fit contractor receives the lead

The highest-scored eligible contractor gets the lead instantly. Founding pros sit at the top of the queue with structural priority. They can accept the job on the homeowner's proposed terms or negotiate directly.
