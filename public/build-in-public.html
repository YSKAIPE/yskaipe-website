Build in Public

Transparent Development and Community-Driven Growth at YSKAIPE

YSKAIPE is committed to open, observable, and participatory development of human-AI collaboration systems. By building in public, progress is shared transparently through live coding, real-time demonstrations, and invitations for aligned developers and contributors to participate directly.

This process accelerates the creation of AI-orchestrated teams — from a single seed prompt to fully executed synergistic workflows — while showcasing core capabilities such as real-time matching, dynamic refinement, and effective human-AI coordination. The approach fosters trust, attracts collaborators, and creates opportunities for partnerships and investment.

Weekly Public Sessions

Two focused public sessions are held each week — Wednesdays and Fridays (4–6 hours each). All are welcome to observe, participate, or contribute.

Wednesday: Core Development & Live Coding

  • Roadmap review: Key priorities for the session (e.g., improving matching algorithms for better alignment on interests and attitudes, developing new pod templates such as "Sustainable App Sprint" or "Ethics Review Pod").
  • Live coding: Refining orchestration logic, integrating specialized tools (e.g., pubchempy for chemistry-focused pods), and implementing real-time monitoring.
  • Open collaboration: Contributors can join via Discord voice or video for pair programming, idea sharing, or taking on specific tasks (e.g., optimizing onboarding flows).
  • Session close: Progress demo, Q&A, and preview of Friday’s session.

Friday: Demonstration, Feedback & Networking

  • Live demonstration: Launch a beta pod from a community-submitted prompt and observe the full orchestration process — matching, team assembly, and execution — in real time.
  • Feedback and iteration: Community polls to prioritize features; open discussion of results, challenges, and adjustments (e.g., performance improvements to matching algorithms).
  • Networking: Conversations with AI professionals, potential collaborators, and interested parties. Metrics (pod success rates, user growth, contributor activity) are shared openly.
  • Recap: Session highlights, clips, and key metrics posted to X (@yskaipe) and LinkedIn.

How to Participate & Stay Updated

  • Live Streams: Available on Twitch or YouTube — search “YSKAIPE Build Session” or follow announcements on X.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Join the public Discord server (link in bio/footer) and enter voice channels during sessions.
  • Code & Roadmap: Access the GitHub repository for shared components (e.g., matching examples, pod templates); view the public roadmap on Notion or Trello.
  • Updates: Weekly summaries of progress, learnings, and metrics posted on X and LinkedIn.

Why Participate

  • Accelerate development through community input and contributions, advancing practical tools for seamless human-AI team formation.
  • Gain visibility into real progress — transparent metrics and live demos provide clear evidence of execution for potential partners and investors.
  • Engage with the broader AI ecosystem: observe advanced orchestration techniques, network with professionals, and contribute to more cohesive human-AI systems.
