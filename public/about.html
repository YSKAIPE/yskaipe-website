Our mission

Price transparency.
Before anyone shows up.

Home services have a price problem. Homeowners get quoted blind. Workers get underpaid or undercut. Platforms profit from the chaos. YSKAIPE was built to fix all three — with a Fair Rate Index that anchors every transaction before it begins.

We're a technology platform that connects homeowners with YSKAIPE Qualified locals — power washers, movers, landscapers, handymen, painters, and more — matched by AI, paid through escrow, and guaranteed by our worker replaceability promise.

Licensed trade pros — HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Roofing, and more — join through our Founding Contractor program, with their own dedicated matching pipeline.

What we believe

The principles behind
everything we build.

Not a manifesto — just the things we come back to every day.

⚖️

Transparency first

The Fair Rate Index exists because homeowners deserve to know what's fair before any worker arrives. Price clarity is a right, not a feature.

🔒

Payment integrity

We hold escrow. Workers are paid only on confirmed completion. Homeowners are never at risk. The money flow is designed around trust, not hope.

🌱

Community roots

We launched in Lake Norman because we live here. Every design decision is tested against what actually works for real neighbors, not hypothetical users.

🔄

Replaceability as a guarantee

If a worker underdelivers, we swap — not argue. Homeowners are never stranded. This is only possible because we control the full transaction.

🤖

AI as a teammate

Claude powers our Fair Rate Index, matching engine, dispatch, and dispute triage. We build with AI the way good contractors build — precisely and without waste.

🌍

Sustainability & stewardship

From Deb's land work to Nick's Bitcoin and garden ethos — we believe in building things that last, consuming less, and leaving places better than we found them.

YSKAIPE is built on Anthropic's Claude — not as a chatbot feature, but as the intelligence layer powering every core function. From the first instant quote to the final payment release, Claude is working.

We believe AI should make markets fairer, not just faster. Every agentic function in YSKAIPE is designed around that principle.
