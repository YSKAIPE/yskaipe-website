The Agreement

The Basic Contract:
Reciprocity + Yield

1

Reciprocity

Provide hands-on help to build, maintain, and care for muscadine setups for 3 other pod members — planting, trellising, pruning, mulching, site visits, harvest support. In return, expect the same from 3 gardeners when you need it.

Balanced, tit-for-tat support. No free riders.

→ Balanced, tit-for-tat support
2

Yield

Share your harvest proportionally with those who helped you — and vice versa. Extra grapes go first to helpers, creating abundance cycles. Could evolve to group processing days, shared preserves, or farmers market collabs.

No cash for core help. Pure reciprocity.

→ Could evolve into shared preserves

🌿 Why Muscadines?

Native to North Carolina, muscadines thrive in heat, humidity, and local soils. They're pest-resistant, full-sun lovers with high antioxidant yields — perfect for fresh eating, jelly, juice, or home ferments. They want to grow here.
