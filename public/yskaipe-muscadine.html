The Agreement

The basic contract:
Reciprocity + Yield

No platforms, no middlemen — just growers helping growers, accountable to each other.

01

Reciprocity

Give hands-on help to 3 other pod members — planting, trellising, pruning, mulching, site visits, harvest support. In return, expect the same from 3 gardeners when you need it.

Balanced, tit-for-tat support. No free riders.

→ Help 3. Receive from 3.
02

Yield

Share your harvest proportionally with those who helped you — and vice versa. Extra grapes go first to helpers, creating abundance cycles. Could evolve to group processing days, shared preserves, or farmers market collabs.

No cash for core help. Pure reciprocity.

→ Could evolve into shared preserves

Why Muscadines

🌿 Native, tough, and built for this climate

Muscadines are native to North Carolina — they thrive in heat, humidity, and local soils. Pest-resistant, full-sun lovers with high antioxidant yields, perfect for fresh eating, jelly, juice, or home ferments. They want to grow here. They just grow better with neighbors.
