Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

At YSKAIPE, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and your rights regarding your data. It applies to our website (yskaipe.com), waitlist sign-ups, platform previews, and any related services (collectively, the “Services”).

By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly
    When you join the waitlist, create an account, fill out forms, or communicate with us, we may collect:
    • Name and email address
    • Profile information (e.g., interests, skills, preferred collaboration style)
    • Any content or messages you submit (e.g., project ideas, feedback)
  • Automatically collected information
    When you visit our site or interact with our Services, we may collect:
    • Device and browser information (IP address, browser type, OS, device ID)
    • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, referral sources, clicks)
    • Cookies and similar technologies (for analytics, functionality, and marketing — see our Cookie Policy below)
  • Information from third parties
    If you connect via third-party services (e.g., Google, GitHub, Slack in the future), we may receive limited profile data with your permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services (e.g., match you with potential collaborators based on attitude and interests)
  • Communicate with you (waitlist updates, product announcements, support responses)
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., recommend relevant project opportunities)
  • Analyze usage trends and enhance platform performance
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in these limited circumstances:

  • With service providers: Vendors who help us operate the Services (hosting, analytics, email delivery, etc.), bound by strict confidentiality
  • For legal reasons: To comply with law, respond to lawful requests, protect rights/safety, or enforce our terms
  • In connection with a business transfer: If we are acquired or merge, your information may transfer (with notice where required)
  • With your consent: When you explicitly agree (e.g., sharing project details within a matched pod)

4. Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Services, fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or meet legal obligations. Waitlist data may be retained until you unsubscribe or we determine it is no longer needed. You can request deletion at any time (see Your Rights below).

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@yskaipe.com. We will respond within the time required by applicable law.

YSKAIPE is based in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. or other countries. We take appropriate safeguards to protect your data in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential functionality, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and performance. You can manage preferences via your browser settings. For more details, see our Cookie Policy [link to future cookie page if created].

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.
