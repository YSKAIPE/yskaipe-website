A mountain retreat in the making

Peaking Waters sits on a steep, beautiful ridge near Blowing Rock/Boone, North Carolina (36°09'50"N 81°39'46"W). Rhododendrons, hardwoods, and sweeping views.

We've been doing all the heavy lifting ourselves — clearing, grading, digging, and building the backbone so this land can become a peaceful private sanctuary and glamping spot.

What We've Built

New Driveways & Roads

Cleared, graded, and connected new driveways and access roads across the steep terrain for year-round usability.

400-Foot Well System

New deep well, pump replacement, and custom wellhouse built to bring reliable water to the ridge.

Terracing & Earthwork

Massive excavation and terracing of the steep slopes to create stable, usable pads and control erosion.

Walkways & Connecting Paths

Hand-built wooden platforms, pallet bridges, and connecting footpaths linking the site together.
